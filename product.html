Built from your data.
Runs like your best employee.

The Business Brain ingests everything your company knows and makes it instantly accessible to everyone on your team.

01

Total Knowledge Ingestion

Upload SOPs, contracts, HR policies, email threads, Slack exports, and spreadsheets. The Brain reads it all and builds a knowledge layer unique to your business.

02

Plain-Language Q&A

Anyone on your team can ask the Brain a question in plain English and get an accurate, sourced answer with a direct link to the exact page it came from.

03

Zero Hallucinations

The Brain only answers from your company's actual documents. It never fabricates information or pulls from the internet unless you specifically ask it to. Every answer is grounded in your data.

04

Employee Onboarding Mode

New hires ask the Brain how things work: processes, policies, who to contact. No more interrupting their manager every five minutes for things that are already documented.

05

Private to Your Business

Your data never leaves your environment. The Brain is trained exclusively on your company's documents and stays entirely within your control.

06

Cited Sources, Every Time

Every answer includes a direct reference to the source document and page it came from, so your team can verify and trust what the Brain tells them.

Who asks the Brain?

Every role in your company benefits from instant access to institutional knowledge.

New Employees

"How do I submit an expense report?" Answered instantly, with a link to the correct form. No supervisor needed for the small stuff.

Customer Support Teams

"What's the warranty on the Model X unit?" The Brain pulls from your product manuals and past tickets to give the rep an accurate, confident answer in seconds.

Sales Representatives

"What case studies do we have for manufacturing clients?" The Brain surfaces relevant win stories, pricing notes, and objection handling from your entire sales history.

Operations Managers

"Show me the SOP for our warehouse receiving process." Find any process document and make sure the whole team is working off the same playbook.

HR & Compliance

"Are we compliant with the updated overtime regulations?" The Brain cross-references your policy documents against compliance guides to flag any gaps.
