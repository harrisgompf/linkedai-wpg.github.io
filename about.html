Our story

It started with one conversation.

One of our co-founders was working a part-time job when a customer found out he studied computer engineering. The moment AI came up — he was hired on the spot to come in and find where automation could help.

That conversation revealed something nobody was talking about: small businesses know they need AI, but have no clear path to getting there. And at the same time, a generation of technically capable graduates can build it — but have nowhere to go.

LinkedAI was built to connect both sides of that gap.
