Taking bookkeeping from 3 days to under 30 minutes

One of five automations we built for a real estate and property management company, and how the same approach works for any business.

23.5h Saved every single month. 3 days of work down to 30 minutes.
98% Faster monthly reconciliation, from 24 hours to 30 minutes
The Challenge

A three-day monthly grind that didn't have to exist

Before working with us, expense reconciliation at Precept Management was a fully manual process. Every month their team spent three full days gathering paper receipts, matching each one against the Visa statement in Excel, calculating GST by hand, and re-entering everything into QuickBooks line by line. It was accurate, but it was an enormous drain on time their team didn't have to spare.

How It Works Now

Four steps. Thirty minutes. Done.

We built an automated workflow that handles the entire reconciliation process from receipt capture to QuickBooks export, with minimal human input required.

STEP 01 Snap & Capture

Receipts drop into a Receipt Library throughout the month with a simple photo snap. Nothing waits for month-end.

STEP 02 Upload & Match

Upload the Visa statement and hit one button. Every transaction is auto-matched to a receipt.

STEP 03 Review & Edit

Transactions are colour-coded red, yellow, or green. GST is calculated automatically. Just review what needs attention.

STEP 04 Reconcile & Generate

One click totals purchases, checks the balance, and exports the Excel & IIF files ready for QuickBooks.
